# Creating an Account

The GSC platform uses Microsoft's secure identity service for authentication. No passwords to remember — just your email address.

---

## How to Log In

1. Click the **Login** link in the top navigation bar
2. You'll be redirected to the secure sign-in page
3. Enter your email address
4. A one-time passcode (OTP) will be sent to your email
5. Enter the code to complete sign-in
6. You'll be redirected back to the GSC website, now logged in

> **First time?** Your account is created automatically when you first log in. No separate registration step needed.

---

## What Changes When You're Logged In

Once authenticated, the navigation bar updates to show additional options:

![Authenticated Navigation](screenshots/07-nav-authenticated.png)

Logged-in users see:
- **My Tickets** — View your event registrations
- **Wiki** — Link to the community knowledge base
- **Logout** — Sign out of your account

If you're a chapter admin, you'll also see:

![Admin Navigation](screenshots/07b-nav-admin.png)

- **Dashboard** — Access the chapter management dashboard

---

## Logging Out

Click **Logout** in the navigation bar. You'll be signed out and redirected to the homepage.

---

## Troubleshooting

- **Not receiving the OTP email?** Check your spam/junk folder. The email comes from `DoNotReply@globalsecurity.community`.
- **Code expired?** OTP codes are time-limited. Request a new one by clicking "Resend code".
- **Need help?** Use the [Contact form](/contact/) to reach the GSC team.
