# Volunteering

We love volunteers! If you'd like to help out at events, you can express your interest when registering.

---

## How to Volunteer

When registering for an event, you'll see a **"I'd like to volunteer at this event"** checkbox.

### Step 1: Express Interest

Check the volunteer interest box. A confirmation section will appear:

![Volunteer Step 1](screenshots/13a-volunteer-step1.png)

### Step 2: Confirm Availability

Read the information about volunteering, then confirm that:
- You're available for the **full duration** of the event
- You understand that not all volunteers can be assigned a role — there are only so many positions available

![Volunteer Step 2](screenshots/13b-volunteer-step2.png)

> **Not sure?** If you're unsure about your availability, we recommend speaking to a chapter lead before committing. You can reach them via the chapter page or at the next event.

---

## What Happens Next

After registering with volunteer interest:
1. Your interest is recorded and visible to chapter admins
2. A chapter lead will follow up if there's a volunteer role available for you
3. If assigned, your role will be updated and you'll gain access to the [QR Scanner](QR-Scanner)

---

## We Appreciate You 💚

Even if we can't offer everyone a volunteer role at every event, we're incredibly grateful to those who offer their time. The community runs on the generosity of people like you. Thank you!

---

## Already Registered?

If you've already registered and want to volunteer, reach out to your chapter lead — they can update your role from the admin dashboard.
