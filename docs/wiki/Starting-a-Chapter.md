# Starting a Chapter

Want to bring the Global Security Community to your city? Apply to lead a new chapter!

---

## How to Apply

1. Go to the **Chapters** page
2. Click **Apply to Lead a Chapter** (or go directly to `/chapters/apply/`)

![Chapter Application Form](screenshots/04b-chapter-apply.png)

---

## Application Form

### Primary Chapter Lead (Required)

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Full Name** | Your full name |
| **Email** | Your contact email |
| **LinkedIn** | Link to your LinkedIn profile |
| **GitHub** | Link to your GitHub profile |

### Second Chapter Lead (Optional)

You can optionally include a co-lead with the same details.

### Chapter Details

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **City** | The city where your chapter will be based (e.g. Perth, London, New York) |
| **Country** | The country |

---

## What Happens After Applying

1. Your application is reviewed by the GSC team
2. If approved, your chapter page is created on the website
3. You'll receive admin access to the [Dashboard](Admin-Dashboard)
4. You can start creating events and building your local community!

---

## Tips for a Strong Application

- **Two leads are better than one** — Having a co-lead shows commitment and resilience
- **Include your LinkedIn** — Helps the team verify your cybersecurity background
- **Pick a city without a chapter** — Check the [Chapters page](/chapters/) to see existing chapters
