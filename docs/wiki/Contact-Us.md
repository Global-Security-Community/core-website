# Contact Us

Have a question, suggestion, or need support? Get in touch with the GSC team.

---

## Contact Form

Visit the [Contact page](/contact/) to send us a message.

![Contact Form](screenshots/05-contact.png)

### Required Fields

- **Name** — Your full name
- **Email** — Where we can reply to you
- **Subject** — Brief topic of your message
- **Message** — Your question or feedback

---

## What Happens

1. Your message is received by the GSC team
2. You'll get a confirmation email at the address you provided
3. We aim to respond within a few business days

---

## Other Ways to Reach Us

- **Wiki** — [wiki.globalsecurity.community](https://wiki.globalsecurity.community) for community knowledge
- **Chapter Leads** — Visit your local [chapter page](/chapters/) for chapter-specific questions
