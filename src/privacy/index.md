  <h1>Privacy Policy</h1>
  <p><em>Last updated: April 2026</em></p>

  <p>The Global Security Community ("GSC", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.</p>

  <h2>1. Data We Collect</h2>
  <p>When you register for an event or interact with our platform, we may collect the following information:</p>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Name</strong> — used to personalise your ticket and communications</li>
    <li><strong>Email address</strong> — used for account authentication, ticket confirmation, and chapter notifications</li>
    <li><strong>Company / organisation</strong> — collected during event registration</li>
    <li><strong>Job title</strong> — collected during event registration</li>
    <li><strong>Industry</strong> — collected during event registration for community insights</li>
    <li><strong>Experience level</strong> — collected during event registration to help us tailor content</li>
  </ul>
  <p>We do not collect payment information. GSC events are free to attend.</p>

  <h2>2. How Your Data Is Stored</h2>
  <p>Your data is stored in <strong>Microsoft Azure Table Storage</strong>, hosted in a Microsoft-managed data centre. Data is encrypted at rest and in transit using industry-standard encryption. Access is restricted to authorised GSC administrators only.</p>

  <h2>3. How Your Data Is Used</h2>
  <p>We use your data solely for the following purposes:</p>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Event management</strong> — generating QR-code tickets, managing attendance, and running check-in at events</li>
    <li><strong>Community communications</strong> — sending transactional emails such as ticket confirmations and event updates</li>
    <li><strong>Chapter notifications</strong> — if you subscribe to a chapter, notifying you of new events in that chapter</li>
    <li><strong>Aggregate analytics</strong> — understanding the makeup of our community (industry, experience level) to improve our programmes. This is never shared in personally identifiable form.</li>
  </ul>
  <p>We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.</p>

  <h2>4. Third-Party Services</h2>
  <p>We use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy practices:</p>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Cloudflare Turnstile</strong> — bot-protection on public forms. Cloudflare may process your IP address and browser signals to verify you are human. See <a href="https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Cloudflare's Privacy Policy</a>.</li>
    <li><strong>Gravatar</strong> — we request a Gravatar image for your email address to display a profile picture. If you do not have a Gravatar, no image is shown. See <a href="https://automattic.com/privacy/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Automattic's Privacy Policy</a>.</li>
    <li><strong>Microsoft Azure AD B2C (CIAM)</strong> — handles account authentication. Microsoft processes your email address to manage your login session. See <a href="https://privacy.microsoft.com/en-us/privacystatement" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Microsoft's Privacy Statement</a>.</li>
    <li><strong>Azure Communication Services</strong> — used to send transactional emails (e.g. ticket confirmations). Your email address is passed to this service solely to deliver the message.</li>
    <li><strong>Discord</strong> — we operate community Discord servers for chapter members. Joining a Discord server is optional and governed by <a href="https://discord.com/privacy" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Discord's Privacy Policy</a>.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>5. Cookies</h2>
  <p>We use a single authentication cookie (<code>StaticWebAppsAuthCookie</code>) set by Microsoft Azure Static Web Apps to maintain your login session. This cookie is strictly necessary for authenticated features and is not used for tracking or advertising purposes. It is removed when you log out.</p>
  <p>We do not use analytics cookies, advertising cookies, or any third-party tracking cookies.</p>

  <h2>6. Data Retention</h2>
  <p>We retain your data for as long as is necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this policy:</p>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Event registrations</strong> — retained for 24 months after the event date to support post-event community activity and reporting.</li>
    <li><strong>Chapter subscriptions</strong> — retained until you unsubscribe.</li>
    <li><strong>Contact form submissions</strong> — retained for 12 months.</li>
  </ul>
  <p>After the applicable retention period, data is deleted from our systems.</p>

  <h2>7. Your Rights</h2>
  <p>You have the right to:</p>
  <ul>
    <li>Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you</li>
    <li>Request correction of inaccurate data</li>
    <li>Request deletion of your data</li>
    <li>Withdraw consent for chapter notifications by unsubscribing at any time</li>
  </ul>
  <p>To exercise any of these rights, please use our <a href="/contact/">contact form</a> and describe your request. We will respond within 30 days.</p>

  <h2>8. Contact</h2>
  <p>For any privacy-related queries, please contact us via our <a href="/contact/">contact page</a>. We take all privacy enquiries seriously and will respond promptly.</p>

  <h2>9. Changes to This Policy</h2>
  <p>We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.</p>
